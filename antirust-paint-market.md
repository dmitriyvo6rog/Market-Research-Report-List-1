<p><ul><li>Physical Antirust Paint</li><li>Chemical Antirust Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antirust paint market is divided into two primary types: physical and chemical antirust paints. Physical antirust paints utilize barriers and coatings to prevent moisture and oxygen from corroding the metal surfaces, often featuring zinc-rich formulations. In contrast, chemical antirust paints contain corrosion-inhibiting compounds that actively react with the surface to prevent rust development. Both types are essential for industrial applications, automotive sectors, and infrastructure maintenance, providing protection against environmental factors that lead to metal deterioration.</p></p>
