<p><p>The Fiber Channel Switch market comprises two primary types: Modular Director Switches and Semi-Modular Switches. Modular Director Switches are high-performance, scalable solutions designed for large data centers, allowing for multiple ports and extensive connectivity options. They offer enhanced flexibility and manageability, suitable for enterprise-critical applications. Semi-Modular Switches, on the other hand, provide a balance between performance and cost, offering fewer ports and simpler scalability for medium-sized environments. Both types cater to diverse connectivity needs in storage area networks.</p></p>
