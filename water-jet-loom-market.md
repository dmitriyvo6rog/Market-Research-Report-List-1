<p><p>The water jet loom market is segmented into two main types: single nozzles and multi nozzles. Single nozzle looms utilize one jet of water to propel the weft yarn, offering simplicity and efficiency for lightweight fabrics. Multi nozzle looms, on the other hand, deploy multiple jets, enabling faster production speeds and greater versatility for a wider range of textile types, including heavier materials. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs, balancing speed, fabric complexity, and production costs.</p></p>
