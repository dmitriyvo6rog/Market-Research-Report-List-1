<p><strong>The Organic Light-emitting Diode (OLED) Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PMOLED</li><li>AMOLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Light-emitting Diode (OLED) materials market is primarily segmented into two types: PMOLED and AMOLED. PMOLED, or Passive Matrix OLED, utilizes a simple drive mechanism, making it cost-effective for smaller displays but less efficient for larger screens. In contrast, AMOLED, or Active Matrix OLED, employs an active matrix of transistors, enabling better brightness, faster refresh rates, and greater efficiency for larger displays, particularly in smartphones and televisions. Both types leverage organic compounds for light emission, enhancing display quality.</p></p>
