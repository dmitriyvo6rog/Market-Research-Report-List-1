<p><ul><li>Ectoparasiticides</li><li>Endoparasiticides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal parasiticide market is primarily divided into two categories: ectoparasiticides and endoparasiticides. Ectoparasiticides target external parasites such as ticks, fleas, and mites, preventing infestations on animals like pets and livestock. They are available in various forms, such as sprays, collars, and topical treatments. Endoparasiticides, on the other hand, address internal parasites, including worms and protozoa. These are typically administered orally or via injection, ensuring the health and well-being of animals by eliminating harmful infestations.</p></p>
