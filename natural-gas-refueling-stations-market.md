<p><strong>The Natural Gas Refueling Stations Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Natural gas refueling stations serve two primary market types: Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) and Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG). CNG stations compress natural gas to store it in high-pressure tanks, primarily used for light-duty vehicles due to their efficiency and lower emissions. In contrast, LNG stations cool natural gas to a liquid state for storage and transport, catering to heavy-duty vehicles and long-haul trucking. Both markets support the transition to cleaner fuel alternatives, reducing reliance on traditional gasoline and diesel.</p></p>
