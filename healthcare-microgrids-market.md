<p><p>The healthcare microgrid market comprises three main types: AC microgrid systems, DC microgrid systems, and hybrid microgrid systems. AC microgrids utilize alternating current to efficiently distribute power, ideal for traditional healthcare facilities. DC microgrids use direct current, often benefiting modern technologies like LED lighting and electronic devices. Hybrid microgrids combine both systems, optimizing flexibility and energy use by integrating renewable sources and storage solutions. Together, they enhance energy reliability and sustainability in healthcare settings, ensuring uninterrupted power supply.</p></p>
