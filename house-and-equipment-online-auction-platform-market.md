<p><p>In the context of a house and equipment online auction platform, there are two primary market types: reserve price and no reserve price. In a reserve price auction, a minimum price is set by the seller, and the item will only be sold if bidding meets or exceeds that threshold. Conversely, in a no reserve price auction, there is no minimum; items are sold to the highest bidder regardless of the final bid amount, often encouraging more aggressive bidding.</p></p>
