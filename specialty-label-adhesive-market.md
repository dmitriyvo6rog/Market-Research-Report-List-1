<p><p>The specialty label adhesive market is primarily divided into water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives utilize water as their primary solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean, suitable for products requiring a clean label approach. Solvent-based label adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong adhesion and durability, ideal for challenging environments or surfaces. Both types cater to various industries, including food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and packaging, each offering distinct advantages depending on application requirements.</p></p>
