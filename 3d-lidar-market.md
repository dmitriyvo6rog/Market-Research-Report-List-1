<p><p>3D-LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) technology uses laser beams to create precise, three-dimensional maps of physical environments. <p><p>The 3D-LiDAR market is categorized into three types based on range: short-range, mid-range, and long-range LiDAR systems. Short-range LiDAR is typically used for detailed mapping of small areas, ideal for applications like robotics and indoor navigation. Mid-range LiDAR serves applications such as autonomous vehicles and surveying, offering a balance between range and detail. Long-range LiDAR is designed for extensive areas, suitable for topographic mapping, environmental monitoring, and large-scale industrial applications, providing extensive coverage with less detail.</p></p>
