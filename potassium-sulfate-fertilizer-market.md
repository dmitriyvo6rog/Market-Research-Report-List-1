<p><p>The potassium sulfate fertilizer market comprises two main types: granule and powder. Potassium sulfate granules are typically used for agricultural purposes, offering slow-release nutrients that improve soil health and crop yield. They are favored for their ease of application and reduced risk of wastage. In contrast, potassium sulfate powder is often utilized in more specific applications, such as in foliar sprays or hydroponics, where quick absorption is essential. Both forms effectively provide potassium and sulfur, essential nutrients for plant growth.</p></p>
