<p><p>The Grounding Busbar market is characterized by two main types: Segregated Bus Duct and Nonsegregated Bus Duct. Segregated Bus Ducts feature separate sections for different circuits, enhancing safety and reducing electromagnetic interference, making them suitable for high-power applications. In contrast, Nonsegregated Bus Ducts combine multiple circuits in a single enclosure, offering a more compact and cost-effective solution, though with increased exposure to electrical faults. Both types serve essential roles in electrical distribution systems, ensuring safe and efficient operation.</p></p>
