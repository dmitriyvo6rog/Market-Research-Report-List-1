<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Fuses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic fuses market is categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I fuses are designed for use in string-level configurations, providing protection for individual modules or strings within a solar array. They ensure safety by interrupting current in case of overloads or short circuits. In contrast, Type II fuses are utilized at the inverter level, protecting the entire system by managing higher voltage and current levels. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing the reliability and efficiency of solar power systems.</p></p>
