<p><p>The B-Hydroxybutyrate assay kits market offers two primary types: fluorometric assays and colorimetric assays. Fluorometric assays utilize fluorescent dyes to measure B-Hydroxybutyrate levels, offering high sensitivity and specificity, making them ideal for low-concentration samples. Colorimetric assays, on the other hand, involve color-changing reactions that indicate B-Hydroxybutyrate levels, providing a straightforward and cost-effective analysis for routine testing. Both methods are valuable in clinical and research settings for monitoring metabolic states and managing conditions like diabetes and ketogenic diets.</p></p>
