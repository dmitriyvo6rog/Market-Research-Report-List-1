<p><p>The monocular indirect ophthalmoscopes market is primarily divided into two types based on illumination technology: halogen lamp illumination and LED illumination. Halogen lamps provide bright, consistent light but generate more heat, potentially causing discomfort during prolonged use. In contrast, LED illumination offers cooler light, longer lifespan, and energy efficiency, making it increasingly preferred in clinical settings. Both types are essential for effective retinal examinations, although the shift towards LED technology is gaining popularity due to its advantages in performance and patient comfort.</p></p>
