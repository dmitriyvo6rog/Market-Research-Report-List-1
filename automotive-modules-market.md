<p><p>The automotive modules market features several package types, including LGA (Land Grid Array), mPCIe (Mini PCI Express), and others. LGA packages are characterized by a grid of pins on the underside, providing robust connectivity for high-performance applications. mPCIe packages are smaller, designed for compact devices with high-speed data transfer capabilities. Other package types may include variant formats that cater to specific automotive requirements, such as durability and size constraints. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing automotive technology and connectivity.</p></p>
