<p><strong>The Sign Sheeting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass bead type</li><li>Micro prismatic type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sign sheeting market is primarily divided into two types: glass bead and micro prismatic. Glass bead sheeting utilizes a layer of glass spheres to reflect light, offering good visibility for standard road signs. Micro prismatic sheeting, on the other hand, employs a more advanced prism technology, which enhances brightness and retroreflectivity, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Both types cater to different regulatory requirements and visibility needs, with micro prismatic sheeting increasingly favored for its superior performance in challenging lighting conditions.</p></p>
