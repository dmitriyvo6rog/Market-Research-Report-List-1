<p><p>The fertilizer additive market comprises various types designed to enhance fertilizer performance and application. Anticaking agents prevent clumping, ensuring easier handling and distribution. Dedusting agents reduce dust during packaging and application, improving user safety. Antifoam agents control foaming in manufacturing processes. Hydrophobic agents enhance water resistance, while corrosion inhibitors protect equipment from damage caused by fertilizers. Other additives may include colorants or nutrient protectors, collectively improving the efficiency, usability, and longevity of fertilizers in agricultural practices.</p></p>
