<p><strong>The SBR Latex Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion SBR Latex</li><li>Solution SBR Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SBR (Styrene-Butadiene Rubber) latex market is primarily divided into two types: emulsion SBR latex and solution SBR latex. Emulsion SBR latex is produced through an emulsion polymerization process, resulting in fine particle sizes, making it suitable for coatings, adhesives, and textiles due to its excellent film-forming properties. In contrast, solution SBR latex is created using a solvent-based method, offering superior strength and resilience, making it ideal for automotive tires and industrial applications. Both types serve distinct market needs.</p></p>
