<p><p>The diagnostic imaging devices market comprises various technologies that aid in medical diagnosis. X-rays provide quick imaging for skeletal and chest evaluations. Ultrasound systems utilize high-frequency sound waves for real-time imaging, ideal for soft tissues and fetal assessments. MRI employs strong magnetic fields and radio waves for detailed internal body images. CT scanners offer cross-sectional views for comprehensive diagnosis. Nuclear imaging systems use radioactive substances to visualize organ function. Additionally, other modalities may include PET scans, fluoroscopy, and more, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
