<p><ul><li>Wireline Logging Evaluation</li><li>Formation Evaluation while Drilling (FEWD)</li><li>Surface Logging Service (SLS)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Logging Evaluation Equipment Market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Wireline Logging Evaluation** involves gathering subsurface data through instruments lowered into wellbores after drilling, providing detailed geological insights. </p><p>2. **Formation Evaluation while Drilling (FEWD)** integrates data collection with ongoing drilling operations, allowing real-time assessment of geological formations.</p><p>3. **Surface Logging Service (SLS)** focuses on monitoring well site activities and surface data collection to interpret the subsurface conditions.</p><p>4. **Other market** encompasses specialized tools and services not classified in the main categories, addressing diverse oil and gas exploration needs.</p></p>
