<p><p>The industrial encapsulation adhesives market is primarily categorized into heat-cured and light-cured adhesives. Heat-cured adhesives require elevated temperatures to initiate curing, providing strong bonds and excellent durability, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, light-cured adhesives use ultraviolet or visible light for rapid curing, offering quick processing times and precision bonding in electronics and medical devices. Both types serve distinct industry needs based on performance criteria, application method, and environmental resistance, enhancing product reliability and lifespan.</p></p>
