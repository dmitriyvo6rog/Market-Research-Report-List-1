<p><p>Carbon-ceramic brake discs are high-performance components used in automotive and motorsport applications, offering superior heat resistance, durability, and lightweight properties. Drilled carbon-ceramic brake discs feature holes to improve heat dissipation, reduce weight, and enhance wet-weather performance by allowing water and gas to escape. Slotted carbon-ceramic brake discs have grooves that improve braking efficiency by clearing debris, maintaining pad contact, and reducing brake fade under high temperatures. Both types cater to performance-driven markets, with drilled discs favoring cooling and slotted discs prioritizing consistent braking under extreme conditions.</p></p>
