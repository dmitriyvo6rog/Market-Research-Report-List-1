<p><ul><li>"Evaporation Equipment"</li><li>"Sputtering Equipment"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR (anti-reflective) coating machine market is primarily segmented into two types: evaporation equipment and sputtering equipment. Evaporation equipment utilizes thermal energy to vaporize coating materials, forming films on substrates, suitable for a variety of optical applications. Sputtering equipment, on the other hand, employs plasma technology to eject material from a target, allowing for precise deposition of coatings, which enhances durability and performance. Both methods are crucial for manufacturing high-quality AR coatings in industries like optics and electronics.</p></p>
