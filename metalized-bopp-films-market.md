<p><p>Metalized BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) films are categorized by thickness into three main segments: 10-30 micron, 30-50 micron, and 50-80 micron. The 10-30 micron films are lightweight and often used for flexible packaging due to their barrier properties. The 30-50 micron range offers a balance of durability and thickness, making it suitable for food and non-food applications. The 50-80 micron films provide enhanced strength and are utilized in heavier packaging solutions, offering superior protection and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
