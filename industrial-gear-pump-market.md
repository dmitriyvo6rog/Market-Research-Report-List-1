<p><strong>The Industrial Gear Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Pumps</li><li>External Gear Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial gear pump market is primarily categorized into two types: internal gear pumps and external gear pumps. Internal gear pumps feature an inner gear rotating within a larger outer gear, creating a vacuum that draws fluid in, ideal for handling viscous fluids and providing smooth, continuous flow. In contrast, external gear pumps consist of two intermeshing gears that push fluid through the pump, making them suitable for applications requiring high pressure and flow rates. Both types are essential in various industries for fluid transfer.</p></p>
