<p><p>The sand blaster market consists of two primary types: dry blasters and wet blasters. Dry blasters utilize compressed air to propel abrasive materials, effectively cleaning surfaces but generating dust and debris. In contrast, wet blasters combine water with abrasive media, minimizing dust and enhancing surface finish while reducing material consumption. Each type caters to specific applications, with dry blasters often preferred for heavy-duty tasks, while wet blasters are favored for delicate surfaces and environmental compliance, promoting safer working conditions.</p></p>
