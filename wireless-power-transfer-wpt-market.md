<p><p>Wireless Power Transfer (WPT) is a technology that enables the transmission of electrical energy without physical connections. It encompasses two main market types: Near-Field Power Transfer, which uses inductive or resonant coupling to transfer energy over short distances, ideal for applications like wireless charging stations; and Far-Field Power Transfer, which employs microwave or laser technology for longer distances, suitable for powering devices like satellites or drones. Both types are pivotal for advancing automation and energy efficiency in various sectors.</p></p>
