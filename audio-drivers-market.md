<p><ul><li>Tweeter</li><li>Midrange</li><li>Woofer & Sub-Woofer</li><li>Super Tweeter</li><li>Crossovers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio drivers market includes various speaker types tailored for different sound frequencies and applications. Tweeters handle high frequencies, providing clarity in treble sounds. Midrange drivers focus on the middle frequencies, essential for vocals and instruments. Woofers produce low tones, enriching the overall sound experience, while subwoofers specialize in deep bass. Super tweeters extend the high-frequency range for enhanced audio detail. Crossovers manage the distribution of sound frequencies between these drivers, ensuring optimal performance in multi-speaker setups.</p></p>
