<p><p>Non-destructive testing (NDT) is a vital process used to evaluate materials without causing damage. The market comprises various types, including Magnetic Particle Testing (MPT), which detects surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields and fine iron particles. Ultrasonic Testing (UT) employs high-frequency sound waves to identify internal flaws and material thickness. Liquid Penetrant Testing (LPT) reveals surface cracks through colored or fluorescent dyes. Other methods include radiographic testing and eddy current testing, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
