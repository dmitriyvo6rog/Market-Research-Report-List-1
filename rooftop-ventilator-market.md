<p><strong>The Rooftop Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Roof Fan</li><li>Centrifugal Roof Fan</li><li>Mixed Flow Roof Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rooftop ventilator market encompasses various types, including axial flow roof fans, centrifugal roof fans, and mixed flow roof fans. Axial flow roof fans move air along the axis of the fan and are ideal for low-pressure applications. Centrifugal roof fans, on the other hand, use a rotating impeller to expel air at high pressure, making them suitable for exhaust and ventilation in larger spaces. Mixed flow roof fans combine features of both types, offering versatility for medium-pressure requirements.</p></p>
