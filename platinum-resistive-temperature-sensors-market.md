<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound Type</li><li>Thin Film Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platinum Resistive Temperature Sensors market is divided into two main types: wire-wound and thin film. Wire-wound sensors consist of a thin wire of platinum coiled around a ceramic or glass core, offering high accuracy and stability for extreme temperatures. In contrast, thin film sensors involve a thin layer of platinum deposited on a substrate, allowing for quicker response times and reduced size. Both types are widely used in industrial applications due to their reliability and precision in temperature measurement.</p></p>
