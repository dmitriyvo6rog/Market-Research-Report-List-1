<p><p>The transit packaging market consists of various types designed to protect and transport goods efficiently. Wooden crates offer sturdy, reusable options for heavy items, while barrels are ideal for liquids and granular substances, providing secure containment. Strapping is used to reinforce packages and secure loads, enhancing stability during transit. Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) are large, reusable containers suitable for storing and transporting bulk liquids and solids. Finally, corrugated boxes are lightweight, versatile, and widely used for packaging various products due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of handling.</p></p>
