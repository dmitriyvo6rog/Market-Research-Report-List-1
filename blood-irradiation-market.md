<p><strong>The Blood Irradiation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Blood Irradiation</li><li>Gamma-ray Blood Irradiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood irradiation market is primarily categorized into two types: X-ray blood irradiation and gamma-ray blood irradiation. X-ray blood irradiation utilizes high-energy X-rays to inactivate pathogens in blood products, enhancing safety for transfusions. In contrast, gamma-ray blood irradiation employs gamma rays from radioactive isotopes, effectively sterilizing blood components by damaging the DNA of potential infectious agents. Both methods are essential for ensuring the safety and efficacy of donated blood, with different applications and regulatory considerations based on their mechanisms.</p></p>
