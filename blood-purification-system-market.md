<p><p>The Blood Purification System market encompasses several key types, including Continuous Blood Purification (CBP), Hemodialysis (HD), and other methods. CBP is a real-time, continuous process ideal for critically ill patients requiring prolonged renal support. Hemodialysis is a more traditional method, performed intermittently to remove waste from the blood in end-stage kidney disease patients. Other methods may include peritoneal dialysis and emerging therapies, broadening the scope of treatment options for renal failure and toxin removal, thereby enhancing patient care.</p></p>
