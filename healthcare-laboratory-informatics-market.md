<p><p>Healthcare Laboratory Informatics encompasses a range of market types that enhance laboratory efficiency and data management. Laboratory Information Management Systems (LIMS) streamline sample tracking and data management. Electronic Lab Notebooks (ELN) replace paper records with digital documentation for research. Scientific Data Management Systems (SDMS) centralize and manage diverse data types from laboratory instruments. Chromatography Data Systems (CDS) focus on the management of chromatography data, while Laboratory Execution Systems (LES) optimize workflow and protocol adherence, integrating processes within the lab environment.</p></p>
