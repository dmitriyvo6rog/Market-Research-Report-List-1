<p><p>The petroleum jelly market is categorized into three main types: pharmaceutical grade, cosmetic grade, and industrial grade. Pharmaceutical grade petroleum jelly is highly refined and meets stringent safety standards for medical applications, including wound care and skin protection. Cosmetic grade is used in personal care products, offering moisture and barrier protection for skin and lips. Industrial grade is less refined and serves various industrial applications, such as lubricants and rust inhibitors, emphasizing functionality and cost-effectiveness over purity.</p></p>
