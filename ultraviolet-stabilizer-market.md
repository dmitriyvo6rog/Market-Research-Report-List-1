<p><p>The ultraviolet stabilizer market includes several key types: UV absorbers (UVA) effectively absorb harmful UV radiation, preventing degradation of materials like plastics and coatings. Hindered amine light stabilizers (HALS) offer long-lasting protection by scavenging free radicals generated by UV exposure, thus providing enhanced durability. Other categories may encompass newer technologies and synergistic formulations that improve performance. Each type plays a crucial role in extending the lifespan and maintaining the aesthetic quality of products exposed to sunlight and environmental stressors.</p></p>
