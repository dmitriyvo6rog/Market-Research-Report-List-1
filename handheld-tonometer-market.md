<p><strong>The Handheld Tonometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Applanation Tonometry</li><li>Indentation Tonometry</li><li>Rebound Tonometry</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Handheld Tonometer market includes various types of tonometry methods. Applanation tonometry measures intraocular pressure by flattening the cornea, providing accurate results. Indentation tonometry involves indenting the cornea with a probe, often used in specific clinical settings. Rebound tonometry utilizes a small, handheld device that measures the rebound of a small probe on the cornea, offering quick and convenient assessments. Other methods may include dynamic contour tonometry and non-contact techniques, catering to diverse clinical needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
