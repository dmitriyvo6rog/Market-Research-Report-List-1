<p><p>The portable soldering iron market is divided into two main types: corded and cordless soldering irons. Corded soldering irons require a direct power source, providing consistent heat and are ideal for extended use. They are often more powerful and suitable for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, cordless soldering irons operate on rechargeable batteries, offering greater mobility and convenience for on-the-go projects. They are typically lighter and easier to handle in tight spaces, making them popular among DIY enthusiasts and professionals alike.</p></p>
