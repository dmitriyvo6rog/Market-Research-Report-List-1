<p><p>The blood transfusion diagnostics market encompasses several testing methods to ensure blood safety. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) detects antibodies in blood samples. NAT (Nucleic Acid Testing) identifies pathogens at a genetic level, enhancing sensitivity. Western Blot Assays confirm the presence of specific proteins. Fluorescence Assays utilize fluorescent markers for enhanced visualization of anomalies. Other technologies may include rapid tests and multiplex assays, broadening the diagnostic capabilities to improve blood transfusion safety and efficacy.</p></p>
