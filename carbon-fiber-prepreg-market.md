<p><p>The carbon fiber prepreg market comprises various types, each with distinct properties and applications. Epoxy prepregs are favored for their mechanical strength and thermal stability, often used in aerospace and automotive sectors. Phenolic prepregs provide excellent fire resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Bismaleimide (BMI) offers superior thermal performance and chemical resistance. Cyanate esters feature low moisture absorption and high thermal stability. Thermoplastic prepregs allow for easier processing and recycling. Other types may include niche formulations for specialized industries.</p></p>
