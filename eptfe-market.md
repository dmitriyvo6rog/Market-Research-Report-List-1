<p><p>The ePTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene) market includes various types such as membranes, sheets, and others. ePTFE membranes are thin, porous materials used for filtration, venting, and separation due to their high permeability and chemical resistance. Sheets are thicker, durable forms used in gaskets, seals, and insulation, offering thermal and mechanical stability. The "others" category encompasses specialized forms like fibers, tapes, and tubes, tailored for specific industrial applications. All types leverage ePTFE's unique properties: hydrophobicity, flexibility, and resistance to extreme conditions.</p></p>
