<p><p>Boat steering wheel pedestals come in various types, each suited for different marine applications. Pinion systems utilize a gear to translate wheel movement into rudder action, offering precise steering. Chain systems connect the wheel to the rudder via a chain, providing a reliable and durable option. Rack-and-pinion setups leverage a toothed bar and gear for efficient steering response. Other types may include hydraulic or electronic systems, catering to specific vessel designs and user preferences, enhancing overall navigation control.</p></p>
