Pure spinning involves using 100% acrylic fibers, resulting in a fabric that exhibits excellent anti-pilling properties and a soft feel. Mixed spinning, on the other hand, blends acrylic with other fibers, such as wool or polyester, to enhance durability and functionality. This diversity allows manufacturers to tailor fabric characteristics for various applications, catering to consumer preferences for comfort, aesthetics, and performance in textiles.</p></p>
