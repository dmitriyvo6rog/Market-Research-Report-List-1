<p><p>The SD-WAN infrastructure market consists of two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises SD-WAN involves deploying hardware and software at the organization’s location, providing greater control and security but requiring significant investment. Conversely, cloud-based SD-WAN leverages a service model where infrastructure is managed remotely, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance efforts. Both approaches cater to varying business needs, allowing organizations to choose the best fit based on their operational requirements and resource availability.</p></p>
