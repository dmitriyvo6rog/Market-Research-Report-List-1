<p><p>The metal forming lubricant market includes water-based, oil-based, semisynthetic, and synthetic types. Water-based lubricants use water as a base, offering cooling properties and eco-friendliness. Oil-based lubricants provide excellent lubrication and are ideal for heavy-duty applications. Semisynthetic lubricants blend oil and water, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness. Synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered for superior performance, high-temperature resistance, and extended tool life. Each type caters to specific metal forming needs, balancing lubrication, cooling, environmental impact, and cost.</p></p>
