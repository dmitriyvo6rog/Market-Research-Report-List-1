<p><p>The composite repairs market is categorized into three main types: structural, semi-structural, and cosmetic. Structural repairs involve significant reinforcement and restoration of a component's strength and integrity, often essential for safety. Semi-structural repairs bridge the gap, providing some reinforcement while allowing for a degree of flexibility in the structure. Cosmetic repairs focus on the appearance rather than the structural integrity, addressing aesthetic concerns without impacting performance. Together, these markets cater to diverse repair needs across various industries.</p></p>
