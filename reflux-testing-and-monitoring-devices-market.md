<p><p>Reflux testing and monitoring devices are essential for diagnosing gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) and related conditions. The market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Reflux Capsule**: A durable, wireless device that measures acid exposure in the esophagus, providing long-term monitoring.</p><p>2. **Reflux Probe**: A flexible catheter inserted through the nose to the esophagus, measuring pH levels continuously.</p><p>3. **Reflux Catheter**: A specialized tube used for direct measurement of acid reflux over a specific period, often in clinical settings. </p><p>Together, these devices enhance reflux assessment and management.</p></p>
