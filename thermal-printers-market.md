<p><p>The thermal printers market comprises two main types: direct thermal and thermal transfer. Direct thermal printers utilize heat-sensitive paper, applying heat to produce images without the need for ink or ribbons, making them ideal for short-term applications like receipts. In contrast, thermal transfer printers employ a heated ribbon to transfer ink onto various materials, providing more durable and high-quality prints suitable for labels and barcodes. Both types serve diverse industries, from retail to logistics, based on specific requirements.</p></p>
