<p><strong>The Military Phased Array Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AESA</li><li>PESA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military phased array antenna market is primarily categorized into two types: Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) and Passive Electronically Scanned Array (PESA). AESA systems feature individual control of each antenna element, enabling rapid beam steering and enhanced performance in tracking multiple targets simultaneously. In contrast, PESA relies on a single transmitter and is generally simpler and less expensive but offers limited beam agility. Both types play crucial roles in advanced radar systems for surveillance, targeting, and communications.</p></p>
