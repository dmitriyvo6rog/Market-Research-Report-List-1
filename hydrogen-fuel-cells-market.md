<p><strong>The Hydrogen Fuel Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-cooled</li><li>Transport-cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen fuel cells market is divided into two main types: air-cooled and transport-cooled systems. Air-cooled fuel cells utilize ambient air for cooling, making them simpler and more cost-effective for stationary applications like backup power. In contrast, transport-cooled systems use active cooling methods, often through liquid coolants, to manage heat in high-performance environments such as vehicles and portable devices. Each type caters to specific operational needs, influencing efficiency, performance, and application suitability in the hydrogen fuel sector.</p></p>
