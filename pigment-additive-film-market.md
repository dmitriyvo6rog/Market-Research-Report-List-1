<p><p>The pigment additive film market is categorized into two main types: inorganic and organic. Inorganic pigments are derived from mineral sources and often display high stability, opacity, and lightfastness, making them suitable for various applications in coatings and plastics. In contrast, organic pigments are carbon-based and are valued for their vibrant colors and versatility. They are widely used in printing and packaging due to their excellent performance characteristics. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing aesthetics and functionality in diverse products.</p></p>
