<p><p>Automated Parcel Terminals (APTs) operate in two market types: one-side and double-side. In a one-side market, parcels are accessed and dropped off from a single side of the terminal, suitable for locations with limited space or specific design needs. Conversely, a double-side market allows access from both sides, enhancing efficiency and reducing wait times, especially in high-traffic areas. Both types cater to the growing demand for automated solutions in logistics, optimizing convenience for users while minimizing land use.</p></p>
