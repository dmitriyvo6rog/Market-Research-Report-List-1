<p><p>The Transport and Industrial Vehicles Glass Market includes laminated and tempered glass types. Laminated glass consists of multiple layers bonded with a plastic interlayer, offering enhanced safety, durability, and sound insulation, making it ideal for windshields. Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength, shattering into small, blunt pieces upon impact, ensuring safety for side and rear windows. Both types cater to vehicle safety standards, with laminated glass prioritizing impact resistance and tempered glass focusing on breakage safety.</p></p>
