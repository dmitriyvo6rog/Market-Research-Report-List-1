<p><p>The shutter sensor market is divided into two primary types: wired and wireless shutter sensors. Wired shutter sensors are connected via physical cables, providing stable and reliable connectivity, making them suitable for fixed installations. These sensors often require professional installation. In contrast, wireless shutter sensors utilize radio frequency or Wi-Fi technology to communicate, offering greater flexibility and easier installation without invasive wiring. They are ideal for retrofit applications and allow for mobile control and convenience, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
