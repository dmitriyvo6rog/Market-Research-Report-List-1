<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Electro-hydraulic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urological operating tables market is categorized into four main types: electric, hydraulic, electro-hydraulic, and manual. Electric tables are powered by motors, allowing precise positioning with minimal effort. Hydraulic tables use fluid pressure for adjustments, offering stable support. Electro-hydraulic tables combine electrical controls with hydraulic mechanisms for enhanced versatility and ease of use. Manual tables rely on manual adjustments, providing a cost-effective solution but requiring more effort from the healthcare staff. Each type caters to specific surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
