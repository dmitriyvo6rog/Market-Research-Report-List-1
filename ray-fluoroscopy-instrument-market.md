<p><p>The Ray Fluoroscopy Instrument market offers two primary types: Remote-Controlled Systems and Patient-Side Control Systems. Remote-Controlled Systems enhance operational efficiency by allowing radiologists to manipulate imaging processes from a distance, minimizing patient exposure to radiation and increasing procedural comfort. Conversely, Patient-Side Control Systems empower healthcare providers to operate imaging equipment directly at the bedside, improving real-time interaction and response with patients. Both systems aim to optimize diagnostic accuracy and enhance patient safety in fluoroscopic procedures.</p></p>
