<p><ul><li>Single Face Brake</li><li>Power off Brake</li><li>Particle Brake</li><li>Hysteresis Power Brake</li><li>Multiple Disk Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electromagnetic brakes market includes various types, each serving unique applications. Single face brakes use a single electrical coil to generate resistance, while power-off brakes disengage when power is cut. Particle brakes utilize magnetic particles for torque modulation, providing smooth control. Hysteresis power brakes rely on magnetic hysteresis for efficient braking performance. Multiple disk brakes combine several disks to improve heat dissipation and braking force. These technologies enhance vehicle safety, control, and efficiency in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
