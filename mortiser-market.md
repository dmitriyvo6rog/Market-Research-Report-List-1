<p><ul><li>Square Chisel Mortiser</li><li>Hollow Chisel Mortiser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mortiser market primarily features two types: square chisel mortisers and hollow chisel mortisers. Square chisel mortisers are designed for creating consistent, square-cut mortises using a solid chisel, ideal for precise woodworking joinery. In contrast, hollow chisel mortisers employ a hollow chisel with a drill bit, allowing for the creation of deeper, cleaner mortises, suitable for more complex joints. Both types cater to different woodworking needs, from fine furniture making to larger-scale production, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in the craft.</p></p>
