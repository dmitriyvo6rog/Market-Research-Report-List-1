<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC card/smart card market consists of two primary types: contact cards and contactless cards. Contact cards require physical insertion into a reader, allowing secure data transfer through direct electrical connections, often used in banking and secure access systems. In contrast, contactless cards utilize radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, enabling transactions by simply holding the card near a compatible reader. This type often enhances convenience, making it popular for public transportation and quick payment solutions in retail environments.</p></p>
