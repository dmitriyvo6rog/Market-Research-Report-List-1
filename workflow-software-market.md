<p><p>The workflow software market can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. In contrast, cloud-based workflow software operates on remote servers, facilitating easy access, scalability, and automatic updates through the internet. This model typically reduces upfront costs and supports collaboration, making it popular among businesses seeking flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
