<p><strong>The Shape Memory Polymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Induced Type</li><li>Electro Induced Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Shape Memory Polymer (SMP) market can be categorized into two main types: heat-induced and electro-induced SMPs. Heat-induced SMPs respond to temperature changes, reverting to a predetermined shape when heated, making them ideal for applications in automotive and medical industries. In contrast, electro-induced SMPs utilize electrical stimuli to achieve shape recovery, allowing for precise control in applications such as soft robotics and wearable devices. Both types offer unique advantages based on their specific activation mechanisms.</p></p>
