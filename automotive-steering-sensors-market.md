<p><ul><li>Contacting</li><li>Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Steering Sensors Market includes two primary types: contacting and magnetic sensors. Contacting sensors utilize physical interfaces to detect the position and angle of the steering wheel, providing accurate data through direct mechanical interaction. In contrast, magnetic sensors employ magnetic fields to sense the steering wheel's position without physical contact, enhancing durability and reducing wear. Both types are crucial for advanced driver-assistance systems, improving vehicle safety and performance by enabling precise steering control and feedback.</p></p>
