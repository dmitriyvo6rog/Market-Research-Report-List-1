<p><p>The planetary gearboxes market includes various types such as linear planetary gearboxes and right angle planetary gearboxes. Linear planetary gearboxes are designed to convert rotational motion into linear motion, often used in applications requiring precision movement. In contrast, right angle planetary gearboxes efficiently change the output axis by 90 degrees, making them suitable for compact designs where space is limited. Both types offer high torque density and efficiency, catering to industries like robotics, automotive, and automation.</p></p>
