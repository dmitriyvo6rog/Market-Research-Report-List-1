<p><p>The freight lifts market is primarily divided into two main types: hydraulic and electric freight lifts. Hydraulic freight lifts utilize fluid pressure to lift heavy loads, offering smooth operation and high lifting capacity, making them ideal for warehouses and industrial settings. In contrast, electric freight lifts use electric motors for operation, providing faster lifting speeds and lower maintenance costs. Both types are designed for heavy-duty applications, enhancing efficiency in transporting goods between different levels in commercial and industrial environments.</p></p>
