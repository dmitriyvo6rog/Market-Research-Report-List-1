<p><p>The industrial gaskets market is categorized into three types: metallic, non-metallic, and semi-metallic gaskets. </p><p>Metallic gaskets are durable and suitable for high-temperature and pressure applications, often made from materials like stainless steel. </p><p>Non-metallic gaskets, typically made from rubber, fiber, or plastics, offer chemical resistance and flexibility, ideal for less demanding conditions. </p><p>Semi-metallic gaskets combine both materials, featuring a metal core with a non-metallic outer layer, providing a balance of strength and sealing capability for various industrial applications.</p></p>
