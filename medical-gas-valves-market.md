<p><p>The Medical Gas Valves market includes Ball Valves, Shut-off Valves, and Check Valves, each serving distinct functions in healthcare systems. Ball Valves regulate gas flow with a rotating sphere, ensuring precise control. Shut-off Valves completely stop or allow gas flow, crucial for safety during maintenance or emergencies. Check Valves permit unidirectional flow, preventing backflow and contamination. These valves are vital for maintaining safe, efficient, and reliable medical gas delivery in hospitals and healthcare facilities, ensuring patient safety and system integrity.</p></p>
