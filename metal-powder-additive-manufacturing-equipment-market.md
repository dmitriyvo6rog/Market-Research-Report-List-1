<p><p>The metal powder additive manufacturing equipment market consists of various types, primarily including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powders layer by layer, allowing for intricate designs and high-resolution components. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powders, offering faster build rates and improved material properties. Other methods may include Binder Jetting and DMLS, expanding the capabilities and applications of metal additive manufacturing technologies.</p></p>
