<p><p>The Split Septum Needleless Connector market is categorized into three main types based on fluid displacement mechanisms: Positive Fluid Displacement, Negative Fluid Displacement, and Neutral Displacement. Positive Fluid Displacement connectors use a mechanism that pushes the fluid forward, minimizing blood reflux. Negative Fluid Displacement connectors create a vacuum effect during use, which can help in reducing blood exposure. Neutral Displacement connectors maintain a balanced pressure, minimizing reflux and air entry, making them suitable for various medical applications in healthcare settings.</p></p>
