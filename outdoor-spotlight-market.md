<p><p>The outdoor spotlight market can be categorized into three main types: recessed, surface mounted, and pendant. Recessed spotlights are installed into ceilings or walls, providing a sleek, unobtrusive look while illuminating outdoor spaces. Surface-mounted spotlights are affixed directly to surfaces like walls or posts, offering versatility and ease of installation. Pendant spotlights hang from above, adding decorative flair while focusing light on specific areas. Together, these types cater to varying aesthetic and functional outdoor lighting needs.</p></p>
