<p><p>The Open Spelter Sockets market consists of two primary types: Cast Steel and Forged Steel Open Spelter Sockets. Cast Steel Open Spelter Sockets are manufactured by pouring molten steel into molds, resulting in strong, complex shapes suitable for various applications. Forged Steel Open Spelter Sockets, on the other hand, are produced by deformation under high pressure, offering enhanced strength and durability. Both types are widely used in industries such as construction, marine, and manufacturing for connecting and securing components.</p></p>
