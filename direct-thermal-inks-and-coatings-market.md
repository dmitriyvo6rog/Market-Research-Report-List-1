<p><p>The Direct Thermal Inks and Coatings market is primarily categorized into two types: water-based inks and coatings, and UV-curable inks and coatings. Water-based inks and coatings are eco-friendly options that utilize water as a solvent, offering excellent adhesion and vibrant colors while minimizing volatile organic compounds. In contrast, UV-curable inks and coatings cure through exposure to ultraviolet light, providing rapid drying times, durability, and resistance to smudging and fading, making them suitable for high-quality printing applications.</p></p>
