<p><p>The Electric Overhead Traveling (EOT) Crane market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Girder EOT Cranes and Double Girder EOT Cranes. Single Girder EOT Cranes feature a single beam that supports the hoist and can efficiently handle lighter loads in limited spaces. In contrast, Double Girder EOT Cranes consist of two beams, allowing for increased lifting capacity and versatility, often suited for heavier loads and larger spans. Both types are essential for various industrial applications, enhancing material handling efficiency.</p></p>
