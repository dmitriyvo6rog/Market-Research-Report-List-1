<p><p>Reflective photoelectric beam sensors are categorized mainly into two types: IR reflective and LED reflective photoelectric beam sensors. </p><p>IR reflective photoelectric beam sensors utilize infrared light to detect objects by measuring the interruption of the beam, making them ideal for low-light environments. In contrast, LED reflective photoelectric beam sensors use visible light, providing enhanced visibility and detection in various conditions. Both types are widely employed in security, automation, and industrial applications for reliable object detection and safety monitoring.</p></p>
