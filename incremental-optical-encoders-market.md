<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>B Type</li><li>Z Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Incremental optical encoders are categorized into three types based on their output signals and applications. Type A encoders provide two-phase output signals (A and B), allowing for direction sensing and precise position feedback. Type B encoders include additional index pulses for improved accuracy in positioning. Type Z encoders, meanwhile, offer a single-phase output, typically for applications requiring simple motion tracking without direction sensing. Each type is tailored to specific industrial needs, balancing complexity and performance to suit various automation tasks.</p></p>
