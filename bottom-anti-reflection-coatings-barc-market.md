<p><p>Bottom Anti-Reflection Coatings (BARCs) are specialized coatings applied to substrates in lithography to reduce reflection and improve resolution during semiconductor manufacturing. The market for BARC is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic. Organic BARCs are composed of polymer materials that offer excellent optical characteristics and are easy to process. Inorganic BARCs, on the other hand, utilize materials like silicon or metal oxides, providing higher thermal stability and superior reflection control, making them suitable for advanced lithographic techniques.</p></p>
