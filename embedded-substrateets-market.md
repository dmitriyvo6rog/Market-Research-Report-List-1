<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embedded Substrate(ETS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Passive Substrate(EPS)</li><li>Embedded Trace Substrate(ETS)</li><li>Embedded Dies Substrate(EDS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Substrate (ETS) market consists of three key types: Embedded Passive Substrate (EPS), which integrates passive components like capacitors and resistors into the substrate for compact design; Embedded Trace Substrate (ETS), which embeds conductive traces directly within the substrate for improved signal integrity and space efficiency; and Embedded Dies Substrate (EDS), allowing semiconductor dies to be integrated within the substrate, facilitating advanced packaging and reducing overall footprint. These innovations enhance performance and miniaturization in electronic devices.</p></p>
