<p><p>The dermal filler market comprises several types, including Hyaluronic Acid (HA), Calcium Hydroxylapatite (CaHA), Poly-L-Lactic Acid (PLLA), and Poly-Methyl Methacrylate (PMMA). HA fillers provide hydration and add volume, while CaHA fillers stimulate collagen production for longer-lasting effects. PLLA promotes collagen synthesis over time, ideal for gradual enhancement, while PMMA offers a semi-permanent solution with microspheres suspended in a gel. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to diverse aesthetic and therapeutic needs in facial rejuvenation.</p></p>
