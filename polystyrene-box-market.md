<p><p>The Polystyrene Box market consists of two primary types: open cell foam and closed cell foam. Open cell foam features interconnected pores, making it lightweight and flexible, often used for insulation and cushioning applications. In contrast, closed cell foam has tightly packed cells that resist moisture and provide better thermal insulation, making it ideal for packaging and protective uses. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, packaging, and refrigeration, enhancing product safety and energy efficiency.</p></p>
