<p><p>Automatic sorting systems are crucial in logistics, retail, and distribution, optimizing the sorting of goods. Cross-belt sorters use belts arranged in a grid to transfer products to their specific destinations, with precise alignment, making them suitable for diverse item sizes and weights. Both systems enhance operational efficiency, accuracy, and throughput in modern supply chain processes.</p></p>
