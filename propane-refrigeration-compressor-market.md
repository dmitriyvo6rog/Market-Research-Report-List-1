<p><ul><li>Piston Compressor</li><li>Screw Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propane refrigeration compressor market comprises primarily two types: piston compressors and screw compressors. Piston compressors utilize a reciprocating mechanism to compress refrigerants, offering high efficiency and adaptability for various applications. They are often favored for smaller systems. Conversely, screw compressors use rotating helical screws to compress propane, providing continuous flow and greater reliability for larger installations. Both types are integral to the refrigeration industry, ensuring efficient cooling solutions for commercial and industrial applications relying on propane as a refrigerant.</p></p>
