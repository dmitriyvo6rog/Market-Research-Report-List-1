<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial digital cameras market primarily consists of two types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras. CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them suitable for high-speed applications. In contrast, CCD cameras typically offer superior image quality and light sensitivity, ideal for precision applications like scientific research. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing, automation, and quality control, each chosen based on specific imaging requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
